Add Twitter share link buttons, follow buttons, hashtag story buttons, or mention buttons to any post or page right from the Visual Editor.

== Description ==

Add as many Twitter buttons to your posts or pages as you want. Choose from a share link, follow button, hashtag for a story, or a mention button. Also choose button size and follow count options. All options can be configured right in your post via the Visual Editor.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `twitter-post-button.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Use the twitter button on your individual post or page to add a button.
